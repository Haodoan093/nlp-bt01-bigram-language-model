BT02 - Evaluation of Two POS Taggers on the Brown Corpus
Run date: 2026-03-14

Dataset:
- Corpus: Brown (NLTK)
- Tagset: universal
- Train/Test split: 80/20
- Number of training sentences: 45,872
- Number of test sentences: 11,468

Results:
1) UnigramTagger
   - Precision (macro): 0.8973
   - Recall (macro):    0.8646
   - Macro-F1:          0.8660
   - Accuracy:          0.9366

2) BigramTagger
   - Precision (macro): 0.9018
   - Recall (macro):    0.8686
   - Macro-F1:          0.8693
   - Accuracy:          0.9455

Conclusion:
- BigramTagger performs better than UnigramTagger on all reported metrics.
- Reason: BigramTagger uses additional context from previous tokens, leading to more stable POS tagging.
